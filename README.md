# Reaper Imbalance
A Balatro mod that is literally unbalanced.

# Warning and Installation
I modified the actual source code, which isn't open source I believe. <br>
If you know how to install this then congrats :>

# Changes
## Jokers: 
### Changed Cards:
(+) Joker - from +4 mult to +8 mult <br>
(+) Jolly Joker, Mad Joker - from +2 mult to +4 mult <br>
(+) Zany Joker - from +3 mult to +6 mult <br>
(+) Crazy Joker, Droll Joker - from +4 mult to +8 mult <br>
(+) Half Joker - from "+20 mult if played <3 card" to "+30 mult if played  <2 cards" <br>
(-) Mystic Summit - from "+15 mult if 0 discards" to "+10 mult if 0 discards" <br>
(+) 8 Ball - from "1 in 4 chance" to "1 in 3 chance" <br>
(+) Misprint - from "0 - 23 mult" to "1 - 24 mult" <br>
(+) Delayed Gratification - from "2$" to "10$" <br> 
(+) Business Card - from "1 in 2 chance" to "1 in 1 chance" <br>
(+) Blue Joker - from "+2 chips" to "+4 chips" <br>
(+) Madness - from "gains x1.25" to "gain x1.5" <br>
(+) Square Joker - from "+4" to "+16" <br>
(+) Riff-Raff - from "adds 2 jokers" to "adds 3 jokers" <br>
(+) Cloud 9 - from "1$" to "2$" <br>
(+) Turtle Bean - from "+5 hand size, -1 per round" to "+10 hand size, -2 per round" <br>
(+) Rough Gem - from "$1" to "$4" | from Uncommon to Rare <br>
(+) Arrowhead - from "+50 chips" to "+100 chips" | from Uncommon to Rare <br>
(+) Onyx Agate - from "+7 mult" to "+21 mult" | from Uncommon to Rare <br>
(+) The Duo - from "x2 mult" to "x4 mult" <br>
(+) The Trio - from "x3 mult" to "x6 mult" <br>
(+) The Order, The Tribe - from "x4 mult" to "x8 mult" <br>
(-) Invisible Joker - from "After 2 rounds" to "After 3 rounds" <br>
